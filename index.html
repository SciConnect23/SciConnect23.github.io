What is Sci-Connect?

Sci-Connect is a one-day Inter Collegiate fest where students from various colleges come together and compete against each other in various events and games in the field of Computer Science,Electronics,Statistics,Mathematics and Biotechnology. Apart from the technical side of the events, students also can participate in non-technical events that do not fall under any field of study, just fun and games! The best participating college will also win a nice attractive award!
